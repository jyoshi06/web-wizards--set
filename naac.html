About University

Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University (DSU) is located in Trichy, Tamil Nadu, and was established under the Tamil Nadu Private Universities Act, 2019. The university is promoted by Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan Educational and Charitable Trust, which has a strong legacy in the field of education.

The university offers a wide range of programs in various disciplines including Engineering, Management, Arts, Science, and Humanities. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty, DSU is committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation and research.

The university is dedicated to the holistic development of students and strives to create an environment that promotes academic excellence, research, and ethical values.

NAAC Accreditation

What is NAAC?

The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) is an autonomous body established by the UGC to assess and accredit institutions of higher education in India.

Importance of Accreditation

NAAC accreditation ensures that the institution meets certain standards of quality in terms of education, infrastructure, research, and teaching-learning process.

Benefits

Accreditation helps institutions in identifying their strengths and weaknesses, and provides guidance for improvement and quality enhancement.
